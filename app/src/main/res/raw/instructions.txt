Classic:

Choose a topic. Answer questions at your own pace, read questions carefully and 
select the answer. Use next and previous button to navigate the quiz and once 
completed, submit the quiz. Review detailed selected solutions at your convenience 
at the bottom of the review screen. Good for beginners who have trouble with concepts
and want to get progress to getting better.

Timed Mode:

A fun challenging way to test your knowledge in calculus. Each topic has an alloted
time to finish the quiz. If unable to finish, the quiz will be automatically submitted
and answers will be revealed. A good way to prepare for timed tests and increase
literacy in the field of calculus.
